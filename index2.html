The flour must be a certain type. Type 00. Itâ€™s the go-to flour for true Neapolitan pizza because it gives you a nice, crispy crust with lots of big airy pockets.

The pizza dough is a basic recipe typical of Italian cuisine. A leavened dough made with flour, water, yeast, oil and salt. Authentic Italian pizza dough must rise for about 4 hours. So you can arrange to prepare it during the day and enjoy it in the evening with the toppings you prefer.

Ingredients(toppings) must be fresh.
Tomato sauce must be also fresh, not cooked and must be prepared with Italian tomatoes, preferably with San Marzano peeled tomatoes, and then blanched with salt, fresh basil and extra virgin olive oil to get an original taste.

Remember that the best Italian pizza is that one straight from the oven.
You should eat it within 5-6 minutes. Wonderful aroma of freshly baked dough will certainly make your pizza disappear in the blink of an eye.
